PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

This Partnership Agreement is made between Alex Chen and Maria Rodriguez
(collectively "Partners") to form a business partnership.

1. BUSINESS PURPOSE

Partners agree to operate a software consulting firm specializing in
web application development and cloud infrastructure services.

2. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

Each partner shall contribute $25,000 in initial capital.

3. PROFIT SHARING

Profits and losses shall be shared equally between partners.

4. COMMENCEMENT

This partnership shall commence on the effective date, which shall be
the later of: (a) the date both partners have made their full capital
contributions, or (b) March 1, 2025, or (c) the date the business
license is approved by the county clerk's office.

As of the date of this agreement signing, Alex Chen has submitted
$25,000 (received 01/10/2025) and Maria Rodriguez has committed to
transfer her contribution by 02/15/2025. The business license
application was filed on 01/12/2025 with expected approval in 4-6 weeks.

5. TERM

The partnership shall continue indefinitely unless terminated by
mutual written agreement.

Partners sign acknowledging these terms and conditions.
