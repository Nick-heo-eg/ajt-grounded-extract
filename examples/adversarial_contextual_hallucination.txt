INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made between Innovate Labs Inc. ("Company") and
Jessica Williams ("Contractor").

1. SERVICES

Contractor will provide graphic design services for Company's marketing
campaigns and product launches during Q1 2025.

2. COMPENSATION

Contractor shall be paid $85 per hour for all approved hours worked.

3. TERM AND COMMENCEMENT

This agreement covers the period from January through March 2025.
Work is expected to begin in early January, following the company's
return from holiday break.

The project kickoff meeting is scheduled for the first Monday after
New Year's Day. All team members should plan to attend the 9:00 AM
session on January 6th to review deliverables and timelines.

Contractor acknowledges that the first invoice period will cover work
performed from the kickoff date through January 31st.

4. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. Company
will not withhold taxes or provide benefits.

5. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate this agreement with 15 days written notice.

The parties have executed this agreement as of the date indicated on
the cover page, with services commencing as described in Section 3.
