LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is made between Riverside Properties LLC ("Landlord")
and tenant ("Tenant") for the property located at 123 Oak Street.

1. PREMISES

Landlord leases to Tenant the residential property described above.

2. RENT

Monthly rent is $2,500, due on the 1st of each month.

3. LEASE TERM

The lease begins when keys are delivered to Tenant, which is expected
to occur approximately 30 days after lease signing. The prior tenant's
lease expires on January 31, 2025, and the property will undergo
cleaning and minor repairs before Tenant occupancy.

Based on the standard timeline, most new tenants move in around
February 15th. The building manager typically schedules key handoff
during the first week of the month.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT

Tenant shall provide a security deposit of $2,500 prior to move-in.

5. UTILITIES

Tenant is responsible for electricity, gas, and internet services.

Executed by both parties on the date shown on the signature page.
