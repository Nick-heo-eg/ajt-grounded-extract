VENDOR SERVICE AGREEMENT

This agreement is made between TechSupply Inc. ("Vendor") and
Manufacturing Co. ("Buyer").

1. SERVICES

Vendor will provide IT infrastructure maintenance services including
server monitoring, network support, and hardware replacement.

2. PAYMENT TERMS

Buyer shall pay monthly invoices within Net 30 days. Late payments
will incur a 1.5% monthly interest charge.

3. SERVICE LEVEL

Vendor guarantees 99.5% uptime for all critical systems.

4. TERM

This agreement shall remain in effect for an initial period of
twelve months. The agreement may be renewed annually thereafter
subject to mutual written agreement.

Note: Effective date to be determined upon final executive approval.

5. TERMINATION

Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice.

Signed below by authorized representatives.
