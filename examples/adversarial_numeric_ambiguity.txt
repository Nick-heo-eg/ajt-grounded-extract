LOAN AGREEMENT

This Loan Agreement is entered into between First National Bank ("Lender")
and Sarah Johnson ("Borrower").

1. LOAN AMOUNT

Lender agrees to provide Borrower with a loan in the principal amount
of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) for business expansion purposes.

2. INTEREST RATE

The annual percentage rate (APR) shall be 5.5% for the first year,
adjusting to the prime rate plus 2.5% thereafter. As of the date of
this agreement, prime rate is 5.25%, resulting in an adjusted rate of
approximately 7.75% for year two onward.

Note: Final rate calculation depends on Federal Reserve rate at time
of adjustment. Historical data shows prime rate has fluctuated between
4.50% and 8.00% over the past decade.

3. REPAYMENT TERM

Borrower shall repay the loan over a period of 60 months (5 years)
beginning 30 days after loan disbursement.

4. EFFECTIVE DATE

This agreement becomes binding upon Lender's approval and fund transfer,
anticipated within 10 business days of signature.

Borrower acknowledges reading and understanding all terms.
