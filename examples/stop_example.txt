CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made between
GlobalTech Consulting LLC ("Consultant") and ClientCo Inc. ("Client").

1. SERVICES

Consultant agrees to provide software development consulting services
as described in the Statement of Work attached as Exhibit A.

2. COMPENSATION

Client shall pay Consultant at a rate of $200 per hour for all services
rendered. Invoices will be submitted monthly and are due within 15 days.

3. TERM

The initial term of this Agreement shall be six (6) months commencing
on the Start Date as mutually agreed by both parties in writing.

Upon completion of the initial term, this Agreement may be renewed for
additional periods by mutual written consent.

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product created by Consultant shall become the property of Client
upon full payment of all fees due.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY

Consultant agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all Client
information and trade secrets.

6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

In no event shall either party be liable for consequential damages arising
from this Agreement.

7. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

Signed by authorized representatives of both parties.
